Breakfast Skillet

There is no better way to start your day than a Skillet with your favorite ingredients

Here are some of mine:

  • Eggs
  • Hash Browns
  • Sausage
  • Green Peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Feel free to add your own ingredients to bring the best flavors.!!

Steps to make a Breakfast Skillet:

  1. Preheat the skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add your favorite ingredients to the skillet.
  3. Cook until the eggs are set and the other ingredients are heated through.
  4. Remove from heat and serve hot.
