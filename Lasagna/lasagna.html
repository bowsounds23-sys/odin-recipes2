Lasagna

Lasagna has a rich attribute of being a hearty and delicious Italian dish. I've grew up with this being one of the most beloved dinner entries in my family. I may even make some tonight.

Ingredients

  • Layered noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Green Peppers
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet.
  4. Add the garlic, green peppers, and onions to the skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
